Epigrafi Latine

Customization of the CLEF application to show the data gathered by the project "Epigrafi Latine", I have worked during my Research Grant at the FICLIT Department of the Unviersity of Bologna.

What I implemented:

  • Conceptual Schema for the data entry.
  • Interactive map to show the resources on place, with filter functionalities.
  • Front-End managemnt of the login and users registration, and customization of users role.
